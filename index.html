About Me

I am a 5th-semester Information Systems student at UPN "Veteran" Jakarta with a strong interest in UI/UX design, web development, data management, and mobile-based applications. Through various academic projects and organizational activities, I have gained hands-on experience as a full-stack developer, covering areas such as user interface design, REST API development, database integration, and CRUD feature implementation. In the field of UI/UX, I enjoy creating intuitive, user-centered designs that enhance usability and user satisfaction. I am familiar with design thinking principles, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing, primarily using tools like Figma. I am also proficient in technologies such as React, PHP, MySQL, Android Studio, Looker Studio, and Google Colab. I have built several applications, including a web-based student management system and a mobile attendance app. I possess strong problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work both independently and in a collaborative team environment. I am eager to deepen my experience and contribute to impactful technology solutions, especially in the areas of UI/UX design and full-stack development.
